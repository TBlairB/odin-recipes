Hot Buttered Rum!

Nothing sets the mood on a cold winter day
better than a roaring fire and a hot buttered
rum. This is a single-serving recipe. If done
right, this drink tastes like melted caramel
swirling over your tongue. That makes it too
sweet to drink with a meal, but also one of
the best dessert drinks ever made. Drink
these in moderation.


Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon of butter

  • 1 teaspoon of brown sugar

  • 1 pinch of ground cinnamon (optional)

  • 1 pinch of ground nutmeg (optional)

  • 1 pinch of allspice (optional)

  • 1 dash of vanilla extract

  • 2 ounces of rum

  • 1 cup of boiling water, or as needed

Directions

  1. Place butter, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg,
    allspice, and vanilla extract in the bottom of
    an irish coffe glass. Pour in rum and hot
    water. Stir.

Nutritional Facts

Per Serving: 195 calories; protein 0.2g;
carbohydrates 6.7g; fat 4.5g; cholesterol
10.8mg; sodium 31.mg.
